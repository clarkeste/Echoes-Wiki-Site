Soak is the ability to endure or reduce [[Damage|Damage]] that would otherwise cause [[Wounds|Wounds]], usually conferred by armor or [[Brawn|Brawn]].

![](https://i.imgur.com/c8zvMsH.png)


Any incoming Damage to a [[Character|Character]] is first reduced by a number equal to their Soak. For example, if your character suffers 6 Damage and has a Soak of 2, they will only suffer 4 Wounds.

To see your Soak at the [[Game Table|Game Table]], first select your character.

![](https://i.imgur.com/DHqF24B.png)

Then, your [[Character Sheet|Character Sheet]] will open at the Game Table. You will be able to see your Soak right at the table and use it for quick reference in a pinch.

![](https://i.imgur.com/JYiGVPU.png)

Make sure to remember to reduce incoming Damage by Soak!
