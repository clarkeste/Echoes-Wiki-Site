Derived Attributes refers to stats, such as [[Wound Threshold|Wound Threshold]], [[Strain Threshold|Strain Threshold]], [[Encumbrance|Encumbrance Threshold]], [[Soak|Soak]], and [[Defense|Defense]] that are not directly bought, but are instead increased or decreased as a results of other stats, such as [[Characteristics|Characteristics]] or [[Talents|Talents]].
