Not to be confused with [[Water Warrior (Modifier)|Water Warrior (Modifier)]].

When a [[Weapons|Weapon]] with this [[Qualities|Quality]] is underwater, has been submerged drenched in water such as from rain within the last two Rounds, its [[Damage|Base Damage]] doubles.
