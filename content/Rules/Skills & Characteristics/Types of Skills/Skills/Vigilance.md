This [[Skills|Skill]] represents a character's ability to take notice of events happening in their surroundings that they are not specifically looking for. It is tied to [[Willpower]], and is a [[General Skills|General Skill]]

It is used in rolls regarding:
- Noticing minor details, such as someone familiar using their off-hand, or a suspicious transaction in plain sight.
- The ability to be aware of your surroundings, often without consciously making an effort.
- Avoiding being deceived.
